The Story

About Interstellar

Born at the seam of mountain nights and city light pollution, Interstellar writes music that feels like standing barefoot on wet earth while watching a meteor shower.

Blending acoustic strings, modular synths, and field recordings from forests and coastlines, each release maps a different latitude of the soul — grief, wonder, and the quiet insistence of growth.

From DIY loft shows to open-air amphitheaters, Interstellar invites listeners into a living planetarium: intimate, luminous, and rooted in the real world.
